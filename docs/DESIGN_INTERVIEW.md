# Design Interview — answer these and I build to spec

You said "interview me." Here it is — grouped so you can answer in passes. Short answers are fine;
"skip" is a valid answer. Everything unanswered, I'll keep deciding with defaults.

## A. Identity (what this game IS)
1. One sentence: a stranger asks what you're making. What do you say — "superhero simulator,"
   "comic-book arena fighter," or something else?
2. Is THRESHOLD the game's name, and Consequences of Failure the universe's? (Title screen says…?)
3. Who is the fantasy for — the player as an ORIGINAL character working up the LeFevre scale, or the
   player piloting your canon Living Superweapons?
4. PvP skirmish, PvE patrol, or co-op vs waves — which one is the heart, the one we polish first?

## B. Combat feel
5. Time-to-KO in a fair 1v1 right now is ~30–60s. Want faster (fighter) or longer (shonen war of attrition)?
6. Haymaker guard-crush: should a crushed defender be COMBO-able (juggle) or just pushed away hard?
7. Should beams "eat" bullets/projectiles that fly into them? (Roadmap #1 — yes/no changes gun balance a lot.)
8. Parry: do you want a tap-guard parry window (adds skill ceiling, adds frustration)? 
9. Energy economy: after Overdrive, is ki still "too generous"? Should base regen drop (9/s → 6/s)?

## C. The scale (LeFevre)
10. Should threat level have GAMEPLAY teeth (Extreme heroes banned from some modes / cost more roster
    points), or is it lore + matchmaking flavor?
11. In a mixed match, should a Low (GALE) realistically beat an Extreme (APEX) on skill — or should the
    scale be honest and lopsided unless the Low player massively outplays?

## D. World
12. First real map: city district, military base, or countryside-with-farms?
13. Pedestrians: score penalty only, or hard fail states ("you leveled the bank, mission failed")?
14. How destroyed can a map get — cosmetic craters (now), buildings collapse (roadmap), or full flatten?
15. Altitude bands (ground/building/sky): hard rule (attacks declare reachable bands) or soft (just aim3)?

## E. Characters
16. KIVULI ships crimson-rose because of your absolute no-purple rule. Override it for him (real purple),
    or keep the rule universal?
17. Next three characters after these twenty — pick from: gravity/black-hole controller, magic mirror
    reflector, Majin-Buu polymorpher, Batman-gadget wall-jumper, sound/scream, plant controller, your own.
18. Custom heroes: is the Character Foundry (JSON drop-in) a NOW priority or after the city map?
19. Voices: generate with your Ad Lab pipeline per-country accents? Any casting notes beyond the doc?

## F. Structure
20. Single-player patrol loop (fly city → incident → arena fight → score): is that the "more than Smash"
    answer you want me to prototype next, or is 2P LAN skirmish first?
